Instructions

Make two teams of at least two people.

A player in team 1 holds the game and clicks start.

He or she then describes each word listed, without using the word itself. Team 1 tries to guess the words. Team 2 remains silent.

When the time runs out team 1 clicks the words they guessed and passes the game to team 2.

Team 2 starts their turn when ready. The team with the highest score after ten rounds wins.
